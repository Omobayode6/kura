Core-Ideation and Sustainability

Kura is a product of an SDGs project, built on the principles of sustainability, resource management, and education. Kura enhances resource recycling amongst students and improve their circular economy and greener environment while supporting academic activities.

This project largely disrupts the traditional means of accessing resources in tertiary institutions which is mainly via “third-party”, “word of mouth” and “posters”. We have created an App (KURA), which is a digital solution that disrupts this traditional process and brought everything (all student’s needs) in one place

Kura brand and our Product

Kura is an Edtech App that offers students access to find resources, supplies, materials and learning support for their academic pursuit. It’s a platform for Students| Teachers| Trainers|Vendors |Coaches| Stakeholders and the general academic community. Kura is your study companion.Project (Kura App) Our MVP Features:E-mart, Freelancing, Accommodation, Bookstore, Scholarships, E-library

Our Mission is simple

Ease student's access for study resources using our digital App which aggregate all resources with improved visibility and reach, hence supporting their learning outcome. We want to create a one-stop App for study resources and learning support.

Core value addition to education

We are committed to a better education and study outcome for our community. Our solution represents needs of the academic environment, and we take pride in contributing to the knowledge and economic wealth of our users.

Kura Traction & Projections

Our bespoke UX research showed that 85% of the respondents wants a platform that can support their learning, and 75% of this audience uses smartphones. Nigeria’s education enrolment data has over 5million students, and Kura is built to support over 2 million users (students, teachers, lecturers, stakeholders, service providers, individuals)

Kura hopes to scale its App development to digitally capture other student’s need areas including exams, maps, online academy, and savings vault. Kura will have, and support over 2 Millions users by 2025 and implement its monetization plan, with possible expansion into West-African regional educational market.
