Kura Student Ambassador Programme (KSAP) is designed for Kura to partner with substantive students in universities, colleges, schools, and polytechnics as Kura representative, and carry our vision and shared value. As a Kura Ambassador, your primary role is to push the idea of Kura in your community and school/schools using all responsible channels.
