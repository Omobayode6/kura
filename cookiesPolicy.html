Cookies policy

This cookie policy explains what cookies we use and how we use them when you use our Service. We recommend that you read the policy fully and your acceptance and/or continued browsing of the service indicates your consent to our use of cookies.

Cookies are small text files containing a unique identifier, which are stored on your computer or mobile device so that your device can be recognised when you're using a particular website or mobile app. When you return to websites – or visit websites that use the same cookies – they recognise these cookies and therefore your browsing device. Some cookies may be used only for the duration of your visit, and others may be used to measure how you interact with services and content over time.

Cookies help to provide important features and functionality on our website and mobile app, and to improve your experience. Cookies can also be used for a variety of reasons, like letting you navigate between pages efficiently, remembering your preferences and generally improving your browsing experience. It also helps us detect fraudulent activity or to prevent security breaches, so we may record information about your device within the cookie. You may not be able to enjoy all functionalities on the platform without these cookies.

The cookies that we use are as follows:

Strictly necessary cookies

These are cookies that are required for the operation of the website and application. These cookies are essential to enable you to move around our platform and use its features, such as accessing secure areas of the website and application. The data collected in this category is essential to provide our services to you. The data is necessary for the website to operate, to verify your identity and to maintain your security and privacy while using the platform. This category is always on, as without this data, the services you’ve asked for can’t be provided.

Functionality cookies

These are cookies that allow our website to remember choices you make (such as your registered name, or the region you are in) and to provide enhanced, more personalised features. These cookies can remember changes you have made to text size, fonts, and other parts of web pages that you can make changes to. They may also be used to provide services you have asked for.

Marketing and analytical cookies

These are cookies that help make our messages more relevant to you. The data informs us about how you use our platforms. We do this to provide you with targeted resources and advertisements that are more relevant to you and your interests. These cookies are used to drive online advertising that is relevant to you by building up a picture of what you are interested in from your use of the internet. The cookies can limit the number of times you see an advert and help to measure the effectiveness of any advertising.

We also collect analytics data related to the use of our services. This allows us to understand how you interact with our services. These insights allow us to both improve content and build better features that enhance your experience.

If you don’t wish to consent to this category, it’s important to note that you may still receive generic advertising or service messages, but they will be less relevant to you and your interests.

We use cookies to:

  • Improve the way our website and mobile app work.
  • Improve the performance of our website and mobile app.
  • Ensure your security and privacy when in our secure sites.
  • Store login details for our secure sites.
  • To recognize you the next time you visit our Service. As a result, the information, which you have earlier entered in certain fields on the Service may automatically appear the next time you use our Service.
  • To speed up the loading of pages.
  • Help us deliver online advertising that we believe is most relevant to you on our websites.
  • Measure the effectiveness of our marketing communications, including online advertising.
  • Help us to understand how you interact with our services. These insights allow us to both improve content and build better features that enhance your experience.

Controlling your Cookies.

You can choose to enable or disable cookies in your internet browser. By default, most internet browsers accept cookies, but this can be changed in your browser settings. You can switch off cookies at any time, however, you may lose information that enables you to access the website more quickly and efficiently.

Changes to this policy.

Kura reserves the right to change this cookie policy as we may deem necessary from time to time or as may be required by law. Any changes will immediately be posted on the platforms and you are deemed to have accepted the terms of the cookie policy on your first use of the platform following the alterations.
