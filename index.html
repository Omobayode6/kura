More Features With Boundless Possibilites

Up-cycle

Up-cycle is your sustainable recycle centre.

Now, students can exchange, recycle, convert and transact their used school items for a token with one another.

With Kura up-cycle, every item has a re-use value, and the meeting point starts here!

scholarship

The scholarships on our platform are tailored to your needs and interest with many options to choose from.

We have fetched the finest scholarships around the globe for you. Just check updates and apply with ease.

beta help

Every little academic support matters in every student's educational pursuit. Beta-help is like freelancer for students.

You can get help with Projects, Assignments and Tutorials classes, and even find experts in several subject areas.

accommodation

Searching for school accommodation is now easy, you can find vacant rooms, hostels and roommates that matches your interest in our App.

This features also allows you to post vacant properties and share roommates in three easy steps.

e-library

Our e-library offers researchers and students access to scholarly resources and available support for their academic works.

Now, you can search through one of the largest collections of online books, journals, articles, and publications from several sources.

bookstore

Kura Bookstore is where you could buy books directly from the Authors, Writers, Book Vendors and other Book authorities.

You can search from our largest collection of numerous titles and connect with the authors and publishers to purchase them.
